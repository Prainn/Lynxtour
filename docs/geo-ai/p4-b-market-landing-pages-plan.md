# P4-B Market Landing Pages Plan

Date: 2026-06-24

Scope: Documentation-only planning for two future Lynxtour market landing pages. No live Shopify pages, templates, sections, snippets, schema code, redirects, navigation links, product data, URLs, handles, canonical tags, robots rules, CSS, JavaScript, booking logic, pricing logic, or visible storefront content were created or changed.

## Planning Guardrails

- Keep both future pages useful for real international travelers, not thin SEO doorway pages.
- Keep Lynxtour positioned as a Yunnan private travel specialist for international travelers broadly, while matching each page's specific intent.
- Keep both pages in a request/confirmation model, not instant checkout.
- Do not duplicate the homepage or broad private tours collection.
- Do not overpromise availability, final pricing, hotel level, vehicle arrangement, guide arrangement, route feasibility, inclusions, exclusions, halal certification, meal availability, or religious-service expertise.
- Use human specialist review language for final route, pricing, hotel, vehicle, guide, meal, inclusion, and exclusion confirmation.
- Use only existing Lynxtour URLs from the safe internal link pool until the future landing pages are actually published.
- Do not use Product or Offer schema unless accurate price or offer data is visible on the page and operationally confirmed.

## Page 1: Yunnan Private Tours from Singapore

Proposed URL:

- `/pages/yunnan-private-tours-from-singapore`

### Page Objective

Who the page is for:

- Singapore-based travelers, expats, families, couples, small groups, and Southeast Asia residents planning a private Yunnan trip.
- Travelers who want a China nature and culture route but need help with destination order, trip length, private transport, guide support, and practical arrival/departure planning.
- Travelers comparing classic Yunnan private tours, custom itineraries, luxury private tours, private day trips, and private car charter.

Search / AI-search intent:

- "Yunnan private tour from Singapore"
- "Yunnan tour package from Singapore"
- "private Yunnan itinerary from Singapore"
- "Kunming Dali Lijiang tour from Singapore"
- "Yunnan family private tour from Singapore"

Business outcome:

- Drive qualified WhatsApp conversations, bespoke itinerary requests, and contact form submissions from Singapore and Southeast Asia travelers.
- Route visitors toward existing Yunnan private tour, luxury private tour, bespoke travel, car charter, and destination pages without creating a country-exclusive brand impression.

### Target User Intent

Primary intent:

- Find a reliable private Yunnan tour planner that can help Singapore-based travelers plan a multi-day route with human confirmation.

Secondary intents:

- Compare 6-day, 8-day, and 10-day Yunnan route options.
- Understand whether Kunming, Dali, Lijiang, Shangri-La, and Xishuangbanna can fit their available dates.
- Ask about private guide, private vehicle, hotel level, family pacing, senior-friendly pacing, meals, and airport/train transfers.
- Understand how to request a custom itinerary before final pricing is confirmed.

Questions users may ask before contacting Lynxtour:

- How many days should Singapore travelers plan for Yunnan?
- Is 6 days enough for Kunming, Dali, and Lijiang?
- Is 8 days better if we want Shangri-La?
- Can Lynxtour customize around school holidays, public holidays, families, or seniors?
- Can we choose hotel level before the final quote?
- Can we use WhatsApp to discuss the itinerary?
- Are prices final online, or do they require confirmation?
- Should we book a private tour, private day trips, or car charter?
