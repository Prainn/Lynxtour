## 1. Purpose of G4

G4 focuses on making Lynxtour a clearer, more consistent brand entity across the open web. The goal is to help travelers, search engines, map platforms, review platforms, and AI answer systems understand who Lynxtour is, what it offers, where it operates, and how travelers can contact the business.

This is not a backlink spam plan. G4 should prioritize accurate business profiles, consistent NAP details, legitimate travel and map platforms, ethical review collection, and citation readiness. Any external profile should help real travelers verify Lynxtour, not exist only for link volume.

## 6. Consistency Rules

- Always use `Lynxtour`.
- Do not use `LynxTour`.
- Do not use different phone numbers unless intentionally tracked and documented.
- Do not use different addresses across platforms.
- Do not choose random or inconsistent service categories. Use tour operator, travel agency, private tour operator, sightseeing tour agency, or the closest platform-supported equivalent.
- Keep descriptions aligned with private and custom Yunnan travel for international travelers.
- Keep destination language consistent: Kunming, Dali, Lijiang, Shangri-La, Xishuangbanna, Lugu Lake and Tiger Leaping Gorge.
- Avoid claiming guaranteed halal availability, guaranteed weather, guaranteed ticket availability, guaranteed hotel outcomes, or guaranteed altitude comfort.
- Do not imply services, destinations or certifications that Lynxtour cannot operationally support.
- Keep profile links pointing to `https://lynxtour.cn/` unless a platform requires a verified alternate URL.

## 7. Review / Trust Signal Plan

- Ask real customers for reviews after trip completion.
- Do not fake reviews.
- Do not write reviews on behalf of customers.
- Do not incentivize reviews in a way that violates platform rules.
- Do not copy the same review across platforms.
- Do not pressure customers to leave only positive reviews.
- Track customer consent before using photos, names, testimonials, videos or identifiable travel stories.
- Keep review requests simple and platform-compliant.
- Where possible, ask customers to mention real trip details naturally, such as route, guide support, pacing, family needs, food planning or destination highlights.
- Keep a private record of review request date, platform, customer consent status and any testimonial usage permission.
