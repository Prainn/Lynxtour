# GEO P3 Content Assets Map

Date: 2026-06-04
Branch: `docs/geo-p3-content-assets-map`
Brand spelling: `Lynxtour`

## Scope

This is a documentation-only plan for the first batch of standalone GEO content assets. GEO P1 answer blocks and GEO P2 comparison blocks have already been implemented and merged. P3 should create deeper traveler-planning articles that help real customers make route, trip length, destination order, private tour style, and special-needs decisions while also giving AI answer systems clear extraction points.

No Liquid code, templates, sections, snippets, JSON-LD, schema, CSS, JavaScript, products, prices, forms, cart, checkout, URLs, handles, canonical, robots, or live content files should be changed in this documentation phase.

## Shared Content Guardrails

- Keep each article focused on one decision problem.
- Include a concise Quick Answer near the top.
- Include at least one comparison table or decision table where useful.
- Give practical route advice, not only attraction descriptions.
- Do not keyword-stuff.
- Do not write generic "Yunnan is beautiful" filler.
- Keep altitude language practical and non-medical.
- Do not claim guaranteed hotel, restaurant, weather, ticket, or altitude outcomes.
- Do not imply fixed prices unless already supported by product pages.
- Use natural CTA guidance, not aggressive sales copy.
- Use only existing Lynxtour internal URLs from the safe link pool unless a future live URL is created intentionally.

For most first-time travelers, 8 days is the most balanced Yunnan itinerary because it includes Kunming, Dali, Lijiang, and Shangri-La without making every day feel rushed. Choose 10 days if you want to add Lugu Lake, slow the pace for family or senior travel, or spend more time in northwest Yunnan. Meili Snow Mountain usually needs a longer and more altitude-aware plan.

| 10 days | Kunming, Dali, Lijiang, Shangri-La, plus Lugu Lake or slower stays | Families, seniors, photographers, repeat China travelers | More flexible, but drive times and destination order matter more. |
| 12-14 days | Classic route plus Meili Snow Mountain or deeper northwest Yunnan | Travelers who want remote landscapes and more buffer time | Higher-altitude and longer-drive planning becomes important. |

Lijiang then works well as the next base for Lijiang Old Town, Jade Dragon Snow Mountain, Tiger Leaping Gorge, and onward travel to Shangri-La. If you have limited time, spend about 2 days in Dali and 2 to 3 days in Lijiang.

| Best for | Slow travel, families, seniors, relaxed scenery | First-time highlights, mountain views, onward routes |
| Suggested time | 2 days for most routes, 3 days if slow-paced | 2 to 3 days, more if adding side trips |
