## 1. Purpose of G5

G5 turns Lynxtour's high-value Yunnan planning content into helpful external answers, social snippets, short-form captions and community-ready response templates. The goal is to meet real traveler questions where they already happen: Reddit, Quora, TripAdvisor forums, Facebook groups, YouTube, Pinterest, Instagram, TikTok and LinkedIn.

This phase is not a spam or link-building automation plan. It should distribute useful route-planning advice from Lynxtour's G3 content assets, create clear brand mentions where appropriate and support external trust signals without fake identities, fake reviews, mass posting or irrelevant link dropping.

## 2. Non-Spam Rules

- Do not pretend to be a neutral traveler if posting as the brand or on behalf of Lynxtour.
- Do not mass-post identical answers.
- Do not drop links where links are not allowed.
- Do not answer unrelated questions just to place a link.
- Do not attack competitors, agencies, destinations or other travelers.
- Do not make medical, visa, weather, ticket, halal, restaurant, hotel, altitude or price guarantees.
- Disclose brand affiliation when appropriate.
- Give useful advice even if the answer does not lead to a sale.
- Adapt each answer to the traveler's route, dates, group type and stated concerns.
- Link only when the link clearly helps the reader and platform rules allow it.
- Prefer no-link answers in stricter communities.
- Do not use automated posting tools or scraped question lists without manual review.
