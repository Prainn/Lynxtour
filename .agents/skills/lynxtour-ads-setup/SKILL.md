Goal:
Help Lynxtour set up paid marketing with clean tracking and practical campaign structure.

Focus:
- GA4 events
- Google Ads conversion actions
- Meta Pixel and CAPI planning
- TikTok Pixel planning
- Shopify checkout and lead tracking
- WhatsApp click tracking
- contact form / inquiry tracking
- draft order / bank transfer flow tracking
- Google Merchant Center product feed issues
- Search Ads, PMax, Meta, TikTok paid social structure

Rules:
- Tracking first, budget second.
- Do not recommend scaling ads before conversion tracking is reliable.
- Separate primary vs secondary conversions.
- Avoid duplicate conversion firing.
- For tourism products, distinguish inquiry, itinerary request, checkout, purchase, WhatsApp click, and email click.

Output:
- What should be tracked
- Event names
- Primary/secondary conversion recommendation
- Platform setup notes
- Risk checklist
- Test plan
